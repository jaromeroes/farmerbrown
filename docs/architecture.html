04 / design_principles
· single entry, many paths
Every caller on every site sees the same first question — "new quote or existing?" — before the flow branches by product. Predictable triage, zero dead ends.
· specialists, not generalists
Each product has its own specialist with its own script, tone, and data requirements. A Builder's Risk caller never feels like they're filling out a Home & Auto form.
· audible handoffs
Receptionists and specialists use distinct voices, so the caller audibly recognizes when they've been connected to the right expert — not just a different prompt.
· graceful escalation
Any caller who is confused, frustrated, or asking for a person is routed to a licensed agent within seconds. The system never insists on automation when a human is the right answer.
